HEART OF DARKNESS

Concept - This portrait depicts a feeling of immense sadness and helplessness of a girl in this harsh society. I tried to show the depth of this problem by converting my thoughts into this charcoal portrait.

Title- Heart of Darkness
Medium- Charcoal
Size- A3

SILHOUETTE STUDY

Concept- This is a Still Life painting of ironing a cloth with deep detailing. It has a neutral color palette of a silk cloth. These Silhouettes give a sense of calm & satisfaction as the cloth falls smoothly.

Title- Silhouette Study
Medium- Acrylic on Canvas
Size- 20 X 18

THE LAMPPOST

Concept- Vintage is one of my favorite aesthetics and I thoroughly enjoyed painting these Victorian style lampposts. I've used a bright color palette to add a vibrant touch to the existing rusty lampposts.

Title- The Lamppost
Medium- Watercolor Paints
Size- A3
