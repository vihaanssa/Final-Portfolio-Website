Typography

FONT DESIGN - JOVIAL

This is a font family that is a display sans-serif typeface with a

Poster & Packaging Design

PRODUCT POSTERS-

Posters re-imagining and inspired from Rococo & Neo-Classical Art
periods. The aim was to identify color palettes, motifs, visual elements
, iconic design features, design principles, etc. from the movements and
apply them to the respective posters.These posters have a minimal
approach in contract to the grandeur of these movements

PICONATE - ITALIAN OLIVE OIL

This packaging design highlights the elegant font ‘Jovial,’
crafted for an authentic Italian olive oil brand. It features a
sleek bottle for daily use and a convenient can refill, blending
timeless tradition with modern aesthetics., creating a visually
appealing and functional packaging solution that captures
the authenticity and premium quality of the product.

Publication Design

MOOD MENU - 7 DISHES 7 MOODS

This beginner-friendly cookbook features seven simple yet flavorful
recipes, each designed to suit a specific emotional vibe. With clear
typography and accessible ingredient illustrations, the
book guides the new cooks for a seamless and easy experience while navigating the recipes.
